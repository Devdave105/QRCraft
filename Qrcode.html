How It Works

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create and share your QR code in under a minute.

1
Select a QR Type

Choose from six QR types: Website URL, Phone Number, WhatsApp Chat, Email Address, Plain Text or WiFi Credentials.

2
Enter Your Information

Fill in the fields for your chosen type. The form validates your input automatically and shows clear error messages if anything is missing or invalid.

3
Customize Color and Size

Use the color pickers to match your brand identity. Choose from four export sizes: 200, 300, 400 or 512 pixels.

4
Click Generate QR Code

Your QR code is generated instantly in the preview panel. No waiting, no loading screens.

5
Download or Copy

Download your QR code as a PNG file for print or digital use. Or copy the encoded data string directly to your clipboard.

6
Share Anywhere

Place your QR on flyers, business cards, social media, websites, packaging or anywhere your customers will see it.
